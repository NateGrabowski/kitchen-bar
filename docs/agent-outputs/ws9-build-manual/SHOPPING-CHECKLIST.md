## Shopping Notes

### Before You Shop

1. **Measure twice:** Verify your space dimensions match the design
2. **Check stock:** Call ahead for specialty items (barn door kit, butcher block)
3. **Order early:** Pre-made doors take 2-3 weeks to arrive
4. **Load help:** Bring a truck/trailer and helper for plywood and butcher block

### At the Store

1. **Inspect plywood:** Check for voids, warping, damage
2. **Check boards:** Look down length for straightness
3. **Get cuts:** Use free cutting service for ripping
4. **Save receipts:** You may need to return/exchange items

### After Shopping

1. **Acclimate:** Store plywood and lumber in work area 48-72 hours
2. **Organize:** Sort materials by project phase
3. **Inventory:** Verify all items against this checklist
4. **Protect:** Keep materials dry and flat
