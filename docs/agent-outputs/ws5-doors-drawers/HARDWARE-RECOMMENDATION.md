**Date:** 2026-01-09

**Work Stream:** WS5 - Doors & Drawers

## Executive Summary

This report provides a complete hardware recommendation for the kitchen bar cabinet project, covering all three sections: drawers (30"), barn door (30"), and double doors (32"). All hardware selections maintain a consistent matte black finish for modern farmhouse aesthetic cohesion.

**Total Estimated Cost:** $315 - $395 (depending on hinge/pull brand choices)

**Key Recommendations:**
- **Barn Door:** JELD-WEN DesignGlide 72" kit ($45-75) - best value with soft-close
- **Hinges:** Blum Compact 38B or Ravinte semi-concealed ($11-32 total)
- **Pulls:** Ravinte 5" bar pulls for drawers, SMARTSTANDARD flush pull for barn door ($40-70 total)
- **Doors:** Pre-made shaker doors from FastCabinetDoors.com ($32-80 for pair)

---

## Cabinet Sections & Hardware Needs

### Section 1: Drawer Section (30" wide)
- **3 drawers** requiring 3 bar pulls (5-6" size)
- **Slides:** Already specified (side-mount soft-close 14")
- **Hardware needed:** Drawer pulls only

### Section 2: Barn Door Section (30" wide)
- **1 sliding barn door** (30" W × 34.5" H)
- **Hardware needed:**
  - Track kit (60" minimum for proper clearance)
  - Soft-stop mechanism
  - Flush pull or vertical handle

### Section 3: Double Door Section (32" wide)
- **2 cabinet doors** (~15.5" W each, overlay style)
- **Hardware needed:**
  - 4 concealed soft-close hinges (2 per door)
  - 2 door pulls (vertical orientation)

## Installation Considerations

### Barn Door Track
- **Track mounting height:** Mount track 1-2" above door height
- **Clearance needed:** Door slides 30" to the left over drawer section
- **Wall preparation:** Requires backing board or blocking behind drywall
- **Soft-close:** JELD-WEN system has integrated soft-close dampers

### Cabinet Hinges
- **Overlay type:** 1/2" full overlay (doors cover face frame edge)
- **Drilling:** 35mm cup hole required for concealed hinges
- **Adjustment:** Blum hinges have 3D adjustment (±2mm each direction)
- **Quantity:** 2 hinges per door (4 total for double door section)

### Drawer Pulls
- **Hole centers:** 5" (128mm) standard - verify drawer face dimensions
- **Mounting:** Center vertically and horizontally on each drawer face
- **Quantity:** 3 total (one per drawer)

### Door Pulls
- **Orientation:** Vertical mount for cabinet doors
- **Height:** Typically 36-40" from floor (upper portion of door)
- **Barn door pull:** Flush mount on sliding door side facing living room

## Finish Consistency Notes

All recommended hardware uses matte black finish, but be aware:
- **Powder coat black** (SMARTSTANDARD, many drawer pulls) - slightly textured
- **Painted/coated steel** (Blum Black Onyx, Liberty) - smooth satin finish
- **Variations:** Slight finish variations between brands are normal

**Recommendation:** Order one sample from each brand before bulk ordering if exact finish match is critical. Most customers find powder coat and painted matte blacks complement each other well.
