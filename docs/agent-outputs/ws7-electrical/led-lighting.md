# LED Lighting Specifications - Kitchen Bar

**Purpose:** LED lighting plan for under-overhang and inside-cabinet illumination
**Date:** 2026-01-09
**Agent:** WS7 Electrical

## Executive Summary

Two LED lighting zones are required:
1. **Under-Overhang LED Strip:** Illuminate living room side from beneath 3" counter overhang
2. **Inside-Cabinet LED Strips:** Illuminate barn door cabinet shelves (2 shelf locations)

Both zones should use 3000K warm white (matches kitchen lighting), and share a common 24V power supply for simplicity.

## Installation Notes

### Sequence

1. **Pre-assembly (before cabinet installation):**
   - Install aluminum channels to cabinet underside/shelf undersides
   - Pre-drill wire routing holes
   - Test-fit strips in channels

2. **During cabinet installation:**
   - Run low-voltage wire through cabinet back
   - Leave excess wire for connections

3. **After cabinet installation:**
   - Mount power supply inside drawer cabinet (accessible)
   - Connect wiring
   - Install LED strips into channels
   - Connect motion sensor in barn door section
   - Test all zones

### Critical Notes

- LED strips have directionality - note arrow direction
- Cut only at marked cut points (every 2")
- Aluminum channel provides heat sink for LED longevity
- Keep power supply accessible for future service
- Use solderless connectors for easy maintenance

## Color Temperature Reference

| Color Temp | Appearance | Best For |
|------------|-----------|----------|
| 2700K | Warm/yellow | Cozy, bedroom |
| **3000K** | Warm white | **Kitchen, bar (SELECTED)** |
| 4000K | Neutral white | Office, task lighting |
| 5000K+ | Cool/daylight | Garage, utility |

**Why 3000K:** Matches typical kitchen overhead lighting. Warm but not yellow. Complements wood tones of butcher block and X-frame accents.
